About

I'm a Data Scientist and Machine Learning Engineer based in Islamabad, Pakistan, with a B.Sc. in Software Engineering and a track record of taking ideas from raw data to production. My work spans classical ML pipelines — preprocessing, feature engineering, model evaluation — and modern applied AI, including building tools powered by large language models.

What sets my approach apart is a bias toward finishing. Rather than stopping at a trained model in a notebook, I build the interface around it, deploy it, and make sure a real person can open a link and get value from it — whether that's a marketing team reading AI-generated campaign insights, or a student getting help writing a thesis.

Outside client and product work, I'm an active builder of personal AI tools and a disciplined systems thinker — currently applying the same rigor I bring to ML pipelines to my own trading strategy research and international graduate school applications.
